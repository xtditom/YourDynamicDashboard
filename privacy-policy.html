Your privacy is the top priority. YourDynamicDashboard does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All your settings, to-do lists, and customizations are saved directly in your browser's local storage and never leave your computer.

üìã Permissions We Request

We only ask for the permissions absolutely necessary for the extension's features to work. Here is a breakdown:

  • storage: This is the primary permission used to save all your custom settings on your own computer. This includes your theme colors, to-do list items, custom shortcuts, and weather API key.
  • geolocation (Optional): This permission is used **only** by the weather widget to automatically find your location for an accurate forecast. Access is managed entirely by your browser, and your location is never tracked or stored.
