How to Get a Free Weather API Key (in 3 steps)

To enable the weather widget in YourDynamicDashboard, you need a free API key from OpenWeatherMap. This guide will walk you through the simple steps to get one.

Step 1: Create an Account

First, you need to sign up for a free account on the OpenWeatherMap website.

  1. Go to the OpenWeatherMap homepage. (Login if you have an account already)
  2. Click on the "Sign in" button, and then find the "Create an Account" link.
  3. Fill in the required details: username, email, password, and accept the terms of service.
  4. Click the "Create Account" button.
  5. Check your email for a verification link and click it to activate your account.Once verified, log in to your account.

Step 2: Find your key and copy

OpenWeatherMap gives you a free API key, you can use that in this extension

  1. After logging in, Click your username located in top-right corner. Then click "My API keys
  2. Now, you can see your API key. Then copy your key.

Step 3: Paste Your Key

Now, just paste your key.

  1. Come back to your new tab page ("YourDynamicDashboard"). Then go to settings panel.
  2. Find the "Weather API Key" input field.
  3. Paste your key into the box and click save.

Important Note

After your key is generated, it might take **a few minutes to a couple of hours**(usually immediately become active) to become active. If the weather doesn't show up immediately, please wait a little while before trying again.

Also, please note that the free plan has a limit of 60 calls per minute. If you exceed this limit, you may experience temporary disruptions in service.

If your API key stop working after a long time, Try to get a new API using another account
